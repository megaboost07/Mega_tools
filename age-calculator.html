How the Age Calculator Works

Our Age Calculator uses precise date arithmetic to compute the difference between two dates. When you enter your date of birth, the tool calculates the exact number of years, months, and days that have elapsed. The algorithm properly accounts for the different number of days in each month, including February's 28 days in common years and 29 days in leap years. This ensures accuracy that manual calculations often miss.

The calculation follows standard chronological aging conventions. Your age in years increments on your birthday each year. The months component represents the number of complete months since your last birthday, and the days component shows how many days have passed since the last complete month. This three-part breakdown gives you the most precise age measurement possible.
